Reaching Keep without Relics

Difficulty★★★★★

Utility/Commonly Used★★★★★

By carefully timing your jumps, you can go from Outer Wall to Clock Tower and then Castle Keep, even though the bridges in the way usually break if you do so. This is because each bridge has a small sweet spot between them where they don’t break, and also you have a small timeframe where you can still jump off a bridge that’s about to break.
