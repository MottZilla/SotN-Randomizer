Flea Ladder

Difficulty★★★★★

Utility/Commonly Used★★★★★

Flea Ladder is one of the master tricks for the game. This trick will allow you to access the Power of Mist check without flight. To do it you need Leap Stone. In this trick you will have to set up the Leap Stone room so that all of the Flea Riders are on screen following you, then, you will start jumping and dive kicking on top of them to gain height, and as you gain height they will also follow, allowing you to do more divekicks for further height. It’s very tricky and it doesn’t come too often as it is risky and can be a considerable time loss if you fail multiple times, but if you’re looking for a new advanced trick to learn, this is for you.
