Leg Day Super Jump

Difficulty★★★★★

Utility/Commonly Used★★★★★

When in the falling air state, if you try to jump as soon as you start to fall, Alucard will do a small “desperate jump” that goes much faster than a regular jump, but with much less horizontal control. This is usually not useful, but in the Leg Day preset where you have unlimited jumps, you can use this mechanic to get extra fast jumps. To do it, walk off a ledge and as soon as you start falling start jumping. These series of jumps will all be “desperate jumps”, which will allow you to move vertically much faster
