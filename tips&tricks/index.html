Summary

In this section we’ll list all of the different tricks and commonly used out of logic checks in races. When we talk about out of logic checks, it means that by doing these tricks you will be able to access certain relics or parts of the castle that you aren’t supposed to access with the relics and items you have. This generally allows racers to get an advantage by acquiring relics that are later in the complexity chain and could lead to big relics like Soul of Bat. In each trick we’ll also include how hard it is to execute, and how commonly used it is, so you can tell which tricks can be useful to learn. These will be scored between 1 and 5 stars.
